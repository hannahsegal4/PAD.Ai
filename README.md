# PAD.AI
**Empowering Homeownership Through Fractional Ownership**

PAD.AI is an AI-driven platform that makes fractional homeownership safe, accessible, and manageable. We match compatible co-buyers, provide legally-vetted ownership agreements, connect users with experts, and offer ongoing co-ownership management.

## Live App
[nesttogether-app.lovable.app](https://nesttogether-app.lovable.app/)

## Market Opportunity
The co-buy real estate market is projected to grow from a 12% niche segment (2018) to 35% of all home sales by 2030 — a nearly $900 billion industry.
